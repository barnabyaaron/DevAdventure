(Story designed and written by Aaron Barnaby 2016)
Enter commands to progress through the story, you can always enter the core commands in the bottom right at any time.
Each room will sometimes have unique commands but your have to work these out, sometimes help can help you.

You wake up in a room with and old man sat on a chair in the corner reading a book, there is one door to the west.
A small kitchen knife lies on a table next to the man.
>

Core Commands

go [direction] (example: go north)
[direction] (example: north or n)
go back - return to previous room
inventory - view held items
look - look around room
help - help text
restart - restart game
